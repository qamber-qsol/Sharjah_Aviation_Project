User Guide for TheRecomm.com

Welcome to Recomm!

Phase 1: Registration

To get started with Recomm, follow these steps:

  1. Register Your Organization: Begin by registering your organization at Recomm Registration. After completing the registration process, we will carefully review your application and send you the login credentials upon confirmation.

Phase 2: Features and Functions

Once registered, you can take advantage of Recomm's powerful features:

  • Link Your Social Media Accounts: Connect your LinkedIn and Twitter accounts seamlessly to unlock the full potential of Recomm.
  • Review Linked Accounts: Verify that your connected accounts are accurately linked and ready for action.
  • Post Content: Utilize Recomm's intuitive Content feature to share compelling content with your audience.
  • Schedule Posts for the Future: Plan ahead by scheduling your posts for optimal timing, ensuring maximum impact.

Rights and Responsibilities

Admin:

After successful registration, the admin should log in using the provided credentials. Follow these steps:

  1. Add Social Accounts: Click on "Add Social" and connect your social accounts. Ensure you create a group before linking your social media.
  2. Group View: After linking social accounts, review them in the group view.
  3. Create Users: As an admin, you can create both managers and members. For members, it's essential to create managers first, as member registration requires a member ID.

Note: Admins are responsible for creating connections and adding users.

Manager:

Managers enjoy the full range of Recomm features. Here's what they can do:

  • Add Social Accounts: Managers can seamlessly add and connect social accounts.
  • Publish Posts on Social Accounts: Managers have the authority to publish posts on connected social media accounts.
  • Schedule Social Media Posts: Schedule posts in advance for strategic and timely content sharing.
  • Approve Member Posts: Managers can approve posts submitted by members.

Member:

Members have limited rights:

  • Create, Publish, and Schedule Content: Members can create, publish, and schedule content, but it requires approval from the manager.

Note: Manager approval is necessary for member-posted content.
